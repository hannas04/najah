About the Author

Abdulbasit ibn Sadiq

Researcher and Educator in Islamic Studies

Abdulbasit ibn Sadiq El-okene is a devoted student of Islamic knowledge, writer, and health educator who strives to connect faith, learning, and service. His journey began with a deep desire to understand the Qur’an and live by the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad through the effort put forth by his parent(Alhaji Sadiq & Hajiya rabiat)and teachers.Guided by the belief that knowledge refines both the mind and soul, Abdulbasit integrates Islamic values into his work as a health educator promoting wellness as a trust from Allah. Through his writings, he reflects on faith, self development, and purposeful living, using words as a means of da’wah and inspiration. He often says, “Knowledge is a trust and writing is a legacy so what ever I learn I must pass on .” With sincerity and humility, Abdulbasit continues to seek knowledge, teach others, and inspire positive change through faith and understanding
